What are IEEE Projects?

IEEE Projects are an initiative within the IEEE Student Branch at UC Berkeley to provide students with the opportunity to explore and increase their technical skills in EE/CS and adjacent fields.

Diversity of fields

The projects cover a wide variety of interests—anything from artificial intelligence to circuitry—so you'll be able to find a project that interests you.

Build and keep a product

Each project member will create their own version of the group project.

Collaborative experience

Each project has their own team of students along with a project manager, so you'll be able to work with others throughout the process.

Low entry barrier

Most of our projects require none to only a basic understanding of EE/CS found in CS61A or EECS16A, and our projects take at most 5 hours a week of time commitment.
